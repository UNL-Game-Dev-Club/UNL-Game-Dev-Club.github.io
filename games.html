Semester Games

Since Fall 2020, students in the UNL Game Dev Club have worked in teams to develop games over the course of each semester. All of the completed games are listed below and most are free to download or play online.

Spring 2022

Otto Bots

Battle a friend for control of an island sinking in the sand in this asymmetric strategy game with local/online multiplayer!

Made by Josh Bellmyer (team lead), Danny Roland, Daniel Riedel, Say Wah, Paul Hennerich, Harry Strong, and Caleb Kirilov.

Download for Windows and Mac on itch.io.

Unfinished Business

A puzzle-platformer where you play as a ghost who is trying to pass on to the afterlife.

Made by Eric Dundas (team lead), Bobby Spatz, Caleb Rosenboom, Sarah Lowderman, and Andrejs Sedriks.

Play online or download for Windows on itch.io.

Exocutioner

A Doom-like first-person shooter, but with wall-walking gravity physics inspired by Super Mario Galaxy.

Made by Ivy Chobyan (team lead), Sam Woods, John Le, Ethan Schmidt, and Lance.

Download for Windows and Linux and get the source code on GitHub.

Globetrotter

A 3D exploration videogame where players puzzle their way through levels on all seven continents in search of the ultimate treasure. Players will learn about the local culture, solve riddles, and decode hidden messages using different types of ciphers.

Made by Ellenna Divingnzzo.

UNL's Education Abroad office is looking forward to partnering with Ellenna to create an adaptation of Globetrotter for students travelling abroad.

Download for Windows on itch.io.

Fall 2021

Things Go Bump in the Night

Can you guide Ted through the mines while avoiding its creature?

Made by Ellenna Divingnzzo (team lead), Ethan Schmidt, Liam Seper, Dylan Wonder, and Junior Wills.

Download for Windows on itch.io.

Tales from the Metahedron

Welcome to the Metahedron, where space is fractured and attacks don't have to happen on the game board!

Made by Sam Woods (team lead), Ivy Chobanyan, Wally Holbeck, Wesley Hill, and Logan Willis.

The team completed a demo over the course of the semester, but it is not publicly available.

Riot Relief

A riot/protest simulator where you can purchase officers, vehicles, and equipment in order to protect an important objective from aggressive individuals breaking the law.

Made by Logan White (team lead) and Thadd Post.

Download for Windows on itch.io.

Levels of Mystery

Play as an apartment building's elevator operator who must solve a murder mystery.

Made by Ben Laube (team lead), Trey Hestermann, Jacob Clouse, Alijah Mallula, Avery West, Carson Haberyan, and Jaevyn Hoyt.

Download for Windows on itch.io.

Spring 2021

When Worlds Kaleid

Cory's world was gray and dull, until he discovered a kaleidoscope that allowed him to peer into a new world!

Made by Josh Bellmyer (team lead), Chase Hermreck, Vinny Nguyen, Long Nguyen, Elliot/Iris Starlin-Hintz, and John Peter Delfosse.

Download for Windows and Mac on itch.io.

Minieval

A tiny medieval city builder inspired by ISLANDERS.

Made by Aaron Friesen (team lead), Kalen Wallin, Viet Ninh, Evan Mielak, Jackson Herman, and Ethan Fox.

Play online, download for Windows, Mac, and Linux, and get the source code on Aaron's website.

Confined

Explore the city of Sarin and delve into the mysteries as to what happened to the many citizens of this small town, all the while wondering if this town is still as dead as it may seem.

Made by Zenith Boerger (team lead), Wally Holbeck, Tyler Senne, Alijah Mallula, and David.

Download for Windows on itch.io.

Giant's Conquest

Play as a giant in search of fame and power. You can accomplish your goals by capturing human villages and slaying the enemy before you!

Made by Samuel Ingledue (team lead), Ben Laube, Logan White, Chris Bayliss, Tristan Nord, and Jaevyn Hoyt.

Play online on itch.io.

Jam Games

Club members have competed in several game jams, which are competitions in which each individual or team makes a game in a short period of time (usually one or a few days).

UNL Game Dev Club Game Jam 2022

The UNL Game Dev Club hosted its first game jam on November 12-13, 2022. The jam ran for 34 hours.

At Large

You are an escaped convict at large, and every 5 seconds you need to make sure you are hidden amongst the crowd. If you aren't 70% covered when the timer runs out your score is reset. How high can you get your score?

Made by Ben Laube.

Play online on itch.io.

UNKNOWABLE

The celestial conjunction is nigh. Assemble your cult and seek out mystical artifacts to summon forth the elder gods, but be wary: the Watch is ever vigilant.

Play online on Aaron's website.

Eye Fell Tower

Made by Josh Bellmeyer.

Dead of Night

Play online or download for Windows and Mac on itch.io.

Out of Mind

Game Maker's Toolkit Game Jam 2020

Whiz Blade

A top-down puzzler where you must escape a tower with a parasitic sword on your arm that can't help but kill whatever is in front of it!

Made by Jaden Goter, Zach Randolph, Matt Lowe, Erik Skoog, and Camden Obertop.

The game placed within the top 2% of the 5,416 submissions. The jam was the largest online game jam ever at the time.

Play online on itch.io. Get the source code on GitHub.

Ludum Dare 46

Squatch Watch

A PS1-style stealth game where you must play as Bigfoot in the forest leaving clues and sneaking past guards to keep the myth of the squatch alive!

Made by Jaden Goter, Grant Davis, Matt Lowe, Erik Skoog, and Camden Obertop.

The game placed within the top 25% of the 4,959 submissions. The jam was the largest online game jam ever at the time.

Play online on itch.io.

K-State Game Jam 2020

Gravity

A platformer where the gravity of the level changes based on the platform you're standing on!

Made by Daniel Shchur, Liam Seper, and Josh Bellmyer.

Play online on itch.io. Get the source code on GitHub.

Flip Trip

Once you reach the top of a level, it flips upside-down and you must play the level again, backwards!

Made by Jaden Goter, Grant Davis, Matt Lowe, and Camden Obertop.

They won second place overall.

Play online on itch.io. Get the source code on GitHub.

Minius Maximus

A turn-based puzzler set in a gladiator high school. You can freely change your stats during combat, but when one stat goes up, another must go down.

Game Maker's Toolkit Game Jam 2019

A puzzle-platformer where you must use four unique items to navigate your way to the end of the level. The twist? You can only hold one item at a time, so you must throw the items around the level to transport them!

The game placed within the top 5% of the 2,606 submissions. The jam was the largest online game jam ever at the time.

Download for Windows on itch.io. Get the source code on GitHub.
