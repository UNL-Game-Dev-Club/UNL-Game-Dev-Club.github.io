Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. We currently do not meet durring the summer, visit the contact page to sign up for updates.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and reach out to one of the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The GDC has some exciting events coming up, which we'd like to share with everyone!

How To: Unity 2D (September 22th)

Don't know how to get your game idea into code? Want to learn a new engine? Come with laptops in hand to learn how to work with Unity 2D on September 22 at 7pm in Avery 19. Attendees will learn the basics of working with 2d in Unity.

Design Planning (September 29th)

Don't even know how to get started with video game design? Hit a roadblock in your game development cycle? Wondering how a simple theme of "light" can turn into an amazing game jam masterpiece? Come to a design strategies meeting on September 29 at 7pm in Avery 19. Attendees will practice designing a video game, focusing on the design strategies and really understanding how to plan things out ahead of time before worrying about implementation.

Chillenium 2019 (October 4-6)

The club will be attending the Chillennium Game Jam in College Station, TX on October 4-6, competing against a multitude of other teams to create a video game in the 3 day period.
