Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. Visit the contact page to get in touch with us or to join the Discord community.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and join the Discord or reach out to one of the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The UNL Game Development Club will be meeting on Sundays from 7PM to 9PM Central. We will be meeting mostly online in our Discord, but will have a couple in-person meetings, so stay tuned for updates. If you are interested in participating, please join the Discord on the contact page.

Game Pitches and Team Organization (September 12th, 7PM Central, IN-PERSON)

Join us on Sunday, September 12th at 7PM as students pitch their games and teams are organized! First, team leads will be announced. Then, students who submitted a game pitch will be able to pitch it to the club. Finally, the team leads will select which game they would like to develop from the pitches and teams will be organized. This meeting will be held in the Johnny Carson School of Emerging Media Arts at 7 PM Central on the first floor, and will be one of our few in-person events. Please remember that UNL currently mandates that you wear a mask indoors. Hope to see you there!
