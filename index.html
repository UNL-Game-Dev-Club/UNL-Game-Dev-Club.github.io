Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club!

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. Visit the contact page to get in touch with us or to join the Discord community.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, you can join our Discord! If you have any other questions, feel free to visit the contact page and reach out to the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The UNL Game Development Club generally meets on Sundays from 7 PM to 9 PM Central Time. We will be meeting mostly online in our Discord, but will have a couple in-person meetings, so stay tuned for updates!

Showcase and Elections (December 5th, 7 PM Central, IN-PERSON at Avery 19)

Join us in-person on Sunday, December 5th at 7 PM as we host a showcase for each of the completed semester projects! Before the showcase, though, we will hold elections for next year's officer team. If you were nominated, officers should reach out to you with more information. After this, teams can present their games and people from the audience can try them out! Finally, the winner of this semester's Best Game award will be revealed. This meeting will be held in person in Avery 19. Anyone is welcome to come and see the club games!
