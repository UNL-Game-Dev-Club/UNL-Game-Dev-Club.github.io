Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. We usually meet in Avery Hall 19 on Sunday nights from 7pm-9pm. Visit the contact page to sign up for updates.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and reach out to one of the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The GDC has some exciting events coming up, which we'd like to share with everyone!

Blender Workshop, Part 1 (April 5th)

Join us on April 5th as we walk through the basics of modeling 3D objects in Blender! We will go over basic modeling, simple texturing/face coloring, and how to export a model for use in Unity 3D. This meeting will be livestreamed from the Voice: General voice chat of our Discord server. Please join the Discord server and download Blender in preparation for the event!

Blender Workshop, Part 2 (April 12th)

Join us on April 12th as we walk through the basics of texturing 3D objects in Blender! We will demonstrate how to texture 3D models and export the materials/textures with their models for use in Unity 3D. We will also be demostrating how to 3D model with voxel in MagicaVoxel, a free voxel-based 3D modeling software. This meeting will be livestreamed from the Voice: General voice chat of our Discord server. Please join the Discord server and download Blender in preparation for the event!
