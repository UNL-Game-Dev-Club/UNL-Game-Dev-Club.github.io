Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. Visit the contact page to get in touch with us or to join the Discord community.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and join the Discord or reach out to one of the club officers.

Information Meeting (August 29th, 6-7PM Central, ONLINE)

Join us on Sunday, August 29th at 6PM as we provide information on what the UNL Game Development Club is all about! We will go over everything that the club does during a typical meeting and show off the games that other club members have made. Anyone is welcome to come learn about the club, and we accept anyone that wants to learn about games and develop them! This meeting will be held on our Discord server in the General voice channel. Please join the Discord server at 6 PM Central if you would like to participate!

How to Design Games (August 29th, 7PM Central, ONLINE)

Join us after the information meeting on Sunday, August 29th at 7PM as we practice designing and pitching games! We will do several game design activities to prepare students to pitch games. After the meeting, the game pitch submission form will open and students can submit their ideas. This meeting will be held on our Discord server in the General voice channel. Please join the Discord server at 7 PM Central if you would like to participate!

Game Pitches and Team Organization (September 12th, 7PM Central, IN-PERSON)

Join us on Sunday, September 12th at 7PM as students pitch their games and teams are organized! First, team leads will be announced. Then, students who submitted a game pitch will be able to pitch it to the club. Finally, the team leads will select which game they would like to develop from the pitches and teams will be organized. This meeting will be held in the Johnny Carson School of Emerging Media Arts at 7 PM Central, and will be one of our few in-person events. Please remember that UNL currently mandates that you wear a mask indoors. Hope to see you there!
