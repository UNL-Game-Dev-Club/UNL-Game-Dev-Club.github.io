Game Jams

Club members have competed in several game jams since the founding of the club. Game jams are competitions in which each individual or team makes a game in a short period of time, usually just a few days. Most game jams have a theme which serves as inspiration.

UNL Game Dev Club Game Jam 2022

In Fall 2022, the UNL Game Dev Club hosted its first game jam! The jam ran for 34 hours, from November 12 to 13, 2022. The theme was "Stay out of sight". All games submitted for the jam can be played below.

At Large

You are an escaped convict at large, and every 5 seconds you need to make sure you are hidden amongst the crowd. If you aren't 70% covered when the timer runs out your score is reset. How high can you get your score?

Made by Ben Laube.

Play online on itch.io.

UNKNOWABLE

The celestial conjunction is nigh. Assemble your cult and seek out mystical artifacts to summon forth the elder gods, but be wary: the Watch is ever vigilant.

Made by Aaron Friesen.

Play online on Aaron's website.

Eye Fell Tower

Navigate through the tower without being seen!

Made by Josh Bellmeyer.

Play online or download for Windows on itch.io.

Dead of Night

Traverse the city without getting caught by zombies!

Made by Eric Dundas.

Play online or download for Windows and Mac on itch.io.

Out of Mind

A game about object permanence, or lack thereof.

Made by Sam Woods and Ivy Chobanyan.

Download for Windows and Linux and get the source code on GitHub.
